Dark Souls Minimalistic Cheat Sheet

  • NG+
  • NG+2-7
  • Information

I'm starting to use this minimalistic checklist after I start to play through NG+.
The checklist is focused on locating missed and important items.
After the first NG+ I'm starting to use second tab where only key items (necessary for completing the game) are listed. Originally made by smcnabb

You can right-click on an item to make it unnecessary to complete the list (and left-click to turn it on again)
